Hook Head Lighthouse

A Journey Through Time on Ireland’s Ancient Coast

Guided Lighthouse Tours

Step inside one of the oldest operational lighthouses in the world and journey through over 800 years of maritime history with our expert guided tours.

Learn about the life of lighthouse keepers, climb the historic tower, and enjoy panoramic views of the rugged Atlantic coastline from the lantern balcony.

Coastal Walks & Wildlife Watching

Experience the dramatic scenery of the Hook Peninsula with our scenic walking trails, ideal for spotting seals, dolphins, and migrating seabirds in their natural habitat.

Interpretive signs along the route offer fascinating insights into the region’s geology, marine life, and ancient folklore.

Visitor Centre & Café

Relax at our visitor centre featuring interactive exhibits, historical displays, and a welcoming café serving locally sourced food and refreshments.

Browse the gift shop for handcrafted souvenirs, books, and memorabilia celebrating the rich heritage of Hook Head Lighthouse.
