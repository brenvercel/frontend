Sligo Animal Rescue

Protecting Animals, Supporting Communities

Animal Rescue & Emergency Response

We provide 24/7 rescue services for injured, abandoned, and mistreated animals throughout Sligo, ensuring they receive immediate care and safety.

Our trained volunteers respond quickly to reports from the public, offering compassionate intervention and transport to our care facilities.

Rehabilitation & Veterinary Care

Every rescued animal receives professional veterinary treatment, nutritious food, and a safe environment to recover physically and emotionally.

We work closely with local vets and specialists to treat injuries, conduct health checks, and prepare animals for adoption or release.

Adoption & Community Outreach

Once animals are rehabilitated, we carefully match them with loving homes through our responsible adoption programme and follow-up support.

We also provide education to the community on animal welfare, responsible pet ownership, and the importance of protecting local wildlife.
